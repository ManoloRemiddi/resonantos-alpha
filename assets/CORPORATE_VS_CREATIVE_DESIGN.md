# Corporate vs Creative Industry Design

## Research Summary (2026-02-01)

### Corporate Design Characteristics
- **Minimal**, clean grid layouts
- Simple, safe typography
- Professional, polished finish
- Muted or conservative color palettes  
- Functional over playful
- Predictable structure
- Trust & stability focused
- **Examples:** Banks, law firms, B2B SaaS, enterprise software

---

### Creative Industry Design (Studios, Artists, Pioneers)
- **Experimental typography** (bold, quirky, oversized, custom)
- **Maximalism** ("more is more") OR strategic minimalism with personality
- **Collage/mixed media** aesthetics
- **Brutalist/Neo-brutalist** influences (raw, unpolished, honest)
- **Hand-drawn elements** - adds human touch
- **Playful & personality-driven** - shows attitude
- **Chaotic/unexpected layouts** - breaks grids
- **Vibrant or unusual color palettes**
- **Retro-futurism** - past meets future
- **Movement/animation** - kinetic typography, subtle animations
- **Analog punk** - neon, collage, chaotic energy

**Examples:** Graphic design studios, artists, music/fashion brands, experimental tech

## 2025-2026 Design Trends for Creative Industry

### Typography
- **Bold, experimental, oversized**
- Typography AS the design (not just supporting)
- Quirky, custom fonts
- Type-only layouts
- Hand-drawn lettering
- 3D typography with textures

### Visual Style
- **Brutalism/Neo-brutalism:** Raw, unpolished, functional-first, bold
- **Maximalism:** Layered patterns, vibrant colors, "more is more"
- **Retro-futurism:** '60s-'90s nostalgia + futuristic tech vibes
- **Analog punk:** Neon colors, collage, chaotic energy
- **Handmade:** Hand-drawn, messy collages, scrapbook-style, riso/screen printing

### Colors
- **Vibrant, bold, unexpected**
- Neon hues (electric blue, magenta, lime green)
- Acid gradients
- Earthy tones (brown, mocha) - Pantone 2025
- High contrast

### Layout
- **Break the grid** - unexpected, asymmetric
- Layered, overlapping elements
- Mixed media (3D + flat, photo + illustration)
- Interactive, animated elements

## ResonantOS Mockup v2 Requirements

### What to KEEP:
- White background (strategic, minimal)
- Black text
- Green accent (#00FF00 for "OS")

### What to CHANGE (Make More Creative):

**Typography:**
- ❌ Standard sans-serif → ✅ Bold, experimental, custom-feeling

- ✅ Mix of type sizes/weights
- ✅ Hand-drawn or rough-edged accent text

- ❌ Perfect grid → ✅ Break the grid slightly
- ✅ Asymmetric placement
- ✅ Overlapping elements

- ✅ Use the 1960s rounded icons more prominently

- ❌ Too polished → ✅ Raw, experimental

**Color Usage:**
- ✅ Mostly black/white (stays minimal)
