# ResonantOS Design Guide

## Visual Theme

**"Pioneering Harmony"** - 1960s futuristic meets organic technology

## Color Palette

### Primary
- **White** `#FFFFFF` - Background (light mode default for public sites)
- **Black** `#000000` - Text, primary elements
- **Green** `#00FF00` - Accent (minimal, strategic use - logo "OS", CTAs, highlights)

### Dark Mode (Watchtower/Admin)
- **Background** `#1a1a1a` - Deep charcoal
- **Text** `#e0e0e0` - Light gray
- **Green Accent** `#4ade80` - Softer green for dark backgrounds

### Status Colors
- **Success** Green variations
- **Warning** Amber `#fbbf24`
- **Error** Red `#f87171`
- **Info** Subtle green or gray

## Typography

- **Headers:** Clean, bold, modern sans-serif
- **Body:** Readable, classic
- **Code/Data:** Monospace

## Visual Language

### Imagery Style
- Black & white photography
- 1960s pioneers in forest settings
- Experimental technology (analog meets digital)
- Parallel timeline concept
- Nature + Machines in harmony

### Icons
- Rounded, 1960s futuristic style
- Black & white with minimal green accents
- Simple, clear, timeless

## Design Principles

1. **Minimalism** - Clean, uncluttered, purposeful
2. **Pioneering Spirit** - Experimental, open, inviting play
3. **Organic Technology** - Human-centric, nature-aware
4. **Retro-Futurism** - 1960s aesthetics meet modern function
5. **Accessibility** - High contrast (black/white), clear hierarchy

## Usage

### Green Accent Guidelines
- **Use sparingly** - Maximum visual impact
- **Use for:** Logo "OS", primary CTAs, active states, key highlights
- **Don't overuse** - Loses impact if everywhere

### Black & White Base
- **Default for:** Text, backgrounds, borders, most UI elements
- **Purpose:** Creates calm, focused, professional feel
- **Contrast:** High readability, accessibility

### Photography
- **Style:** Black & white, wide angle, documentary feel
- **Subject:** Pioneers + technology, nature + machines
- **Mood:** Hopeful, experimental, harmonious

## Website Hierarchy

1. **Public Sites** (DAO, marketing) - White background, minimal green
2. **Watchtower/Admin** - Dark mode, subtle green accents
3. **Widget** - Adapts to host site, "Powered by ResonantOS" in green
